Corrections in the Preposition

01. 'Investigate'- এর পরে কোনাে Preposition বসে না। তাই এখানে 'into' বসবে না।

Inc.: The magistrate investigated into the matter.
Cor.: The magistrate investigated the matter.


02. 'Confident'- এর পরে of বসে 'Confident of'- এর অর্থ আস্থাশীল।

Inc.: I am confident on your prosperity.
Cor.: I am confident of your prosperity.

 

03. 'Attend to'- এর অর্থ মনােযােগ দেয়া।

Inc.: I attend my studies regularly.
Cor.: I attend to my studies regularly.


04. চোখে না দেখার ক্ষেত্রে 'Blind of' এবং দোষ না দেখার ক্ষেত্রে 'Blind to' বসে।

Inc.: He is blind to his eyes.
Cor.: He is blind of his eyes.

Inc.: He is blind of his son's fault.
Cor.: He is blind to his son's fault.

 

5. Order, resign, reach, resemble, regret, emphasize, discuss, sign ইত্যাদি verb- এর পরে কোনাে preposition বসে না।

Inc.: They discussed about the matter.
Cor.: They discussed the matter.

Inc.: He ordered for a glass of water.
Cor.: He ordered a glass of water.

Inc.: She resigned from the post.
Cor.: She resigned the post.

Inc.: They reached at the bus stand.
Cor.: They reached the bus stand.

Inc.: He resembles to his father.
Cor. He resembles his father.

06. 'Fond of'- এর অর্থ অনুরাগী। তাই fond- এর পরে for না বসে of বসে।

Inc.: I am fond for my father.
Cor.: I am fond of my father.

07. কারাে সাথে প্রতিযােগিতা করার ক্ষেত্রে Compete with/against বসে এবং কোনাে কিছুর জন্য প্রতিযােগিতা করার ক্ষেত্রে Compete for বসে।

Inc.: Babu is competing to me for the post.
Cor.: Babu is competing with/against me for the post.

08. 'Stick to'- এর অর্থ ধরে রাখা বা লেগে থাকা। তাই stick- এর পরে at না বসে to বসেছে।

Inc.: You should stick at your principles.
Cor.: You should stick to your principles.


9. 'Prohibit' এবং 'Refrain'- এর পরে from বসে। 'From' preposition হওয়ায় এর পরে Gerund (verb + ing) বসে।

Inc.: My brother prohibited me to do the work.
Cor.: My brother prohibited me from doing the Work.

Inc.: He will refrain to do it.
Cor.: He will refrain from doing it.


10. সাধারণত, উদ্দেশ্য বুঝালে To Infinitive বসে। এখানে সে হাসপাতালে গিয়েছিল মাকে দেখার উদ্দেশ্যে। তাই for- এর পরিবর্তে to Infinitive বসবে।

Inc.: She went to hospital for seeing her mother.
Cor.: She went to hospital to see her mother.


11. Senior, junior, superior, inferior, preferable, prior ইত্যাদি Adjective নিজেরাই Comparative বলে তাদের পূর্বে more বসে না এবং এদের পরে than না বসে to বসে।

Inc.: He is more junior than me.
Cor.: He is junior to me.

Inc.: Milk is more preferable than tea.
Cor: Milk is preferable to tea.


12. সে পায়ে হেঁটে কলেজে গিয়েছিল। সে অন্যের পায়ে কলেজে যেতে পারে না। অতএব foot- এর পর্বে his লিখে foot কে determined করার প্রয়ােজন নেই।

Inc.: He went to college on his foot.
Cor: He went to college on foot.

 

Misuse of Preposition

 

Preposition- এর ব্যবহারের ক্ষেত্রে আমরা বার বার ভুল করি। নিচে preposition- এর কিছু ব্যবহার লক্ষ্য করুন.

Inc.: Open page fifty.
Cor.: Open at page fifty.

Inc.: He meditates his past life.
Cor.: He meditates upon his past life.

Inc.: He repents his past conduct.
Cor.: He repents of his past conduct.

Inc.: I believe God.
Cor.: I believe in God.

Inc.: No one should boast his wealth.
Cor.: No one should boast of his wealth.

Inc.: His conduct admits no excuse.
Cor.: His conduct admits of no excuse.

Inc.: How are you going with your studies?
Cor.: How are you going on with your studies?

Inc.: You should compensate him the loss.
Cor.: You should compensate him for the loss.

Inc.: You should attend your lessons.
Cor.: You should attend to your lessons.

Inc.: We dispensed his services.
Cor.: We dispensed him with his services.

Inc.: He refers me in the letter.
Cor.: He refers to me in the letter.

Inc.: I appeal you for help
Cor.: I appeal to you for help.

Inc.: I can not account his failure.
Cor.: I can not account for his failure.

Inc.: He arrived the station.
Cor.: He arrived at the station.

Inc.: Can you guess at her age?
Cor.: Can you guess her age?

Inc.: Don't jeer a lame man.
Cor.: Don't jeer at a lame man.

Inc.: Listen my lecture.
Cor.: Listen to my lecture.

Inc.: The baby pointed the bird.
Cor.: The baby pointed to the bird.

Inc.: I defer you on this point.
Cor.: I defer with you on this point.

Inc.: He cheated the man his dues.
Cor.: He cheated the man of his dues.

Inc.: He complied my request.
Cor.: He complied with my request.

Inc.: This pen belongs me.
Cor.: This pen belongs to me.

Inc.: I cannot approve your conduct.
Cor.: I cannot approve of your conduct.

Inc.: He always counts my help.
Cor.: He always counts on my help.

Inc.: I take him a fool.
Cor.: I take him for a fool.

Inc.: He is devoid common sense.
Cor.: He is devoid of common sense.

Inc.: I blamed him what he had done.
Cor.: I blamed him for what he had done.

Inc.: A drowning man catches a straw.
Cor.: A drowning man catches at a straw.

Inc.: He dwells a fine house.
Cor.: He dwells in a fine house.

Inc.: The surgent operated the patient.
Cor.: The surgent operated upon the patient.

Inc.: He disposed his house.
Cor.: He disposed of his house.

Inc.: I am unable to cope him.
Cor.: I am unable to cope with him.

Inc.: He resembles to his father.
Cor.: He resembles his father.

Inc.: They discussed about the matter.
Cor.: They discussed the matter.

Inc.: He resigned from the post.
Cor.: He resigned the post.

Inc.: They met with me yesterday.
Cor.: They met me yesterday.
Inc.: He reached in safely.
Cor.: He reached safely.

Inc.: This violates against the rule.
Cor.: This violates the rule.

Inc.: He ascended on/up the hill.
Cor.: He ascended the hill.

Inc.: I ordered for his dismissal.
Cor.: I ordered his dismissal.

Inc.: He contradicted against my opinion.
Cor.: He contradicted my opinion.

Inc.: The boy picked up a quarrel with him.
Cor.: The boy picked a quarrel with him.

Inc.: The police investigated into the matter.
Cor.: The police investigated the matter.

Inc.: This does not answer to my purpose.
Cor.: This does not answer my purpose.

Inc.: I emphasise on the matter.
Cor.: I emphasise the matter.

Inc.: I shall inform to your father.
Cor.: I shall inform your father.
