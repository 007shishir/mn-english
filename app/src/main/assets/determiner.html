The Determiners

Determiners:

যেসব word কোনাে noun বা pronoun - এর পূর্বে বসে তাকে নির্দেশ করে তাদেরকে Determiner বলে।

Classification of determiners:
Determiners তিন প্রকার। যথা:

1. Central Determiner:

Articles: ইংরেজি ভাষায় সবচেয়ে বেশি ব্যবহৃত determiner হলাে definite and indefinite article (a, an, the).
Runa is a student.
The man is good.

Demonstratives: This, that, these, those.
This book is interesting.
That mango is ripe.

Possessives: My, our, your, his, her, their, etc.
We love our country.
I want your book.

Quantifiers: Some, any, no, enough, either, neither, much, many, each, every, etc.
I have enough rice.
Do you have any book?


2. Predeterminer:

যে determiner গুলাে central determiner - এর পূর্বে বসে তাদেরকে predeterminer বলা হয়।
both, all, half, double, one-third, such, what, etc.
I want double the amount.


3. Postdeterminer:

যে determiner গুলাে central determiner - এর পরে বসে তাদেরকে postdeterminer বলে।

Cardinal Numbers: one (a), two, three, four, etc.
I like the three books.

Ordinal Numbers: first, second, third, etc. Karim is the first boy.
March is the third month of the year.

Closed-class Quantifiers: few, large, etc.
I have a few books.

Open-class or Phrasal Quantifiers: lot of, deal of, amount of, great deal of, etc.
He has a great deal of money.
