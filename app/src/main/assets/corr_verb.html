Corrections in the Verb

01. পাপ করার ইংরেজি commit a sin। তাই পাপ করা বােঝাতে do- এর পরিবর্তে commit বসবে।

Inc.: He did a sin.
Cor.: He committed a sin.


02. জড় পদার্থ ডুবা sink এবং জীব ডুবা drown.

Inc.: The boy sank.
Cor.: The boy drowned.
Inc.: The ship drowned.
Cor.: The ship sank.


03. নাড়ি দেখা যায় না, অনুভব করতে হবে। তাই নাড়ি দেখার অর্থে see- এর পরিবর্তে feel বসবে।
Inc.: The doctor saw my pulse.
Cor.: The doctor felt my pulse.


04. 'Lest'- এর পরে সাধারণত should/might বসে।

Inc.: He ran fast lest he miss the bus.
Cor.: He ran fast lest he should miss the bus.


05. Because of/In-spite of- এর পরে সাধারণত noun/noun phrase/gerund (verb + ing) বলে।

Inc.: He became very tired because of do the work.
Cor.: He became very tired because of doing the Work.

Inc.: He did not prosper in life in-spite of work hard.
Cor.: He did not prosper in life in-spite of working hard.


06. কোনাে বাক্যে বিভিন্ন person থাকলে প্রথমে 2nd person, পরে 3rd person এবং শেষে 1st person বসে (সংক্ষেপে, 231)। কিন্তু দোষ বা খারাপ কাজ বুঝালে প্রথমে 1st person, পরে 2nd person এবং শেষে 3rd person বসে (সংক্ষেপে, 123)।

Inc.: I, he and you will go there.
Cor.: You, he and I will go there.
Inc.: You, he and I am guilty.
Cor.: I, you and he are guilty.

 

 

Misuse of Verbs


Inc.: We say him a liar.
Cor.: We call him a liar.

Inc.: The doctor saw his pulse.
Cor.: The doctor felt his pulse.

Inc. He always tells the truth.
Cor.: He always speaks the truth.

Inc.: The boy spoke a lie.
Cor.: The boy told a lie.

Inc.: Please see my certificate.
Cor.: Please look at my certificate.

Inc.: He talks good English.
Cor.: He speaks good English.

Inc.: He will give the examination.
Cor.: He will appear at the examination.

Inc.: See the Scripts.
Cor.: Look over the script.

Inc.: He gave a good speech.
Cor.: He delivered a good speech.

Inc.: He did a crime/sin/fault.
Cor.: He committed a crime/sin/fault.

Inc.: See the word in the dictionary.
Cor.: Look up the word in the dictionary.

Inc.: He gave me goodbye.
Cor.: He bade me goodbye.

Note: কাউকে বােকা বা মিথ্যাবাদী বলা call, রােগীর নাড়ী দেখা feel, পরীক্ষা দেয়া appear at, খাতা দেখা look over, বক্তৃতা দেয়া deliver, পাপ করা commit, dictionary- তে শব্দ খোজা look up, certificate দেখা look at, মিথ্যা বলা tell, সত্য বলা speak, ভুল করা make a mistake/error.

Inc. He denied to help me.
Cor: He refused to help me.

Inc.: He refused that he did not take the money.
Cor.: He denied that he did not take the money.

Note: কোনােকিছু অস্বীকার করা refuse এবং সত্য অস্বীকার করা deny.

Inc.: He gave me a visit.
Cor: He paid me a visit.

Inc.: I caught his hand.
Cor.: I caught him by the hand.

Inc.: He loves to play cricket.
Cor: He likes to play cricket.

Inc.: I like my son.
Cor: I love my son.

Inc.: I expect to succeed.
Cor.: I hope to succeed.

Inc.: I hope it will take a month.
Cor.: I expect it will take a month.

Inc.: The boy took his birth in a poor family.
Cor: The boy was born in a poor family.

Inc.: I shall await for you.
Cor.: I shall await you.

Inc.: The boy took admission in to the college.
Cor.: The boy got himself admitted into the college.

Inc.: He knows to swim.
Cor. He knows how to swim.

Inc.: I shall mend my pencil.
Cor.: I shall sharpen my pencil.

Inc.: He hopes he will not pass.
Cor.: He is afraid he will not pass.

Inc.: We made two goals.
Cor.: We scored two goals.

Inc.: He forwarded himself as a candidate.
Cor. He offered himself as a candidate.

Inc.: I intend to visit America.
Cor.: I wish to visit America.

Inc.: He tells that he will go home.
Cor.: He says that he will go home.

Inc.: He told me, "Do the work."
Cor.: He said to me, "Do the work."

Note: Indirect object ছাড়া quotation এর পূর্বে tell verb ব্যবহৃত হয় না। Say verb এর indirect object থাকলে উক্ত object এর পূর্বে to বসে।
